Browse freely

Fear Filter runs automatically on every page you visit. Detected images are blurred with an overlay — click any blurred image to reveal it if you choose.

How it works

Instant text scan — image descriptions, alt text, captions, and filenames are checked first.
Local AI vision — images without descriptions are analyzed by a CLIP model that runs entirely in your browser. The model (~170 MB) downloads once on first use, then works offline.
Completely private — your images and choices are never sent to any server. Everything runs on your device.
