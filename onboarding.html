Welcome to Fear Filter

AI-powered image filtering that automatically detects and hides
images of the things that bother you most.

1

Get a free Anthropic API key

Fear Filter uses Claude AI to analyze images. You'll need your own API key from console.anthropic.com — it's free to sign up. Checking images costs roughly $0.001 per image (one tenth of a cent).

2

Paste your key into Fear Filter

Click the Fear Filter icon in your Chrome toolbar, paste your API key (starts with sk-ant-) into the field, and hit Save.

3

Choose what to filter

Select any combination of fears to block. Fear Filter supports:

🐍 Snakes 🕷️ Spiders 🤡 Clowns 💉 Needles 🩸 Blood 🏔️ Heights 🦈 Sharks 🐀 Rats & Mice 🪳 Cockroaches 🐝 Wasps & Bees 🪆 Dolls & Puppets 👁️ Eyes Close-up
4

Browse freely

Fear Filter runs automatically on every page you visit. Detected images are blurred — click any blurred image to reveal it if you choose.

Your API key is stored locally on your device and never sent anywhere except directly to Anthropic.
